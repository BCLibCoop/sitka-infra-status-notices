The BC Libraries Cooperative will be upgrading our instance of Evergreen from version 2.6 to version 2.8 from Saturday, July 11th at 9:00PM PDT 
(10:00PM MDT, 11:00PM CDT) through Monday, July 13th at 5:00AM PDT (6:00AM MDT, 7:00AM CST). The Evergreen servers and public catalogue will be unavailable 
during the upgrade. For more information see the <a href="https://bc.libraries.coop/support/upgrade/">Sitka Evergreen 2.8 Upgrade</a> webpage.
